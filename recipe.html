How to make Fettuccine Carbonara


Ingredients:

Dough

    ~ 2 Cups of flour (plus extra for dusting)
    ~ 2 Whole eggs, plus one yolk
    ~ Some olive oil (optional)

Sauce, for a serving

    ~ 1/3 Cup grated cheese (or more)
    ~ 1 Whole eggs, plus one yolk
    ~ Meat of choices, chopped into smaller pieces (Smoked beef, Sausages, etc)
    ~ Salt, pepper, and oregano

Tools:

    ~ Forks
    ~ Bowls
    ~ Measuring cups
    ~ Pasta machine
    ~ Plastic wrap
    ~ Cheese grater
    ~ Pot or Pan
    ~ Stovetop
    ~ Serving Plate and cutlery

Optional tools:

    ~ Scraper and Tongs

Steps:

Dough

  • 1.) Prepare your tools and ingredients (make sure to wash your hands before doing this activity
  • 2.) Pour 2 cups of flour to a clean work surface, make a deep nest in the middle of it with a bowl
  • 3.) Crack all the eggs, pour them into the well (along with olive oil). Start whisking the eggs and combine them with the flour (start from the edge)
  • 4.) After the dough is well combined, it should be dry enough to touch. Start kneading the dough.
  • 5.) Knead well until smooth and not sticky, then wrap it in plastic wrap
  • 6.) Rest the dough, about 30 min-4 hours
  • 7.) Unwrap the dough,cut into 4 pieces, take one (dust with flour) and stretch it a little bit
  • 8.) Dust the pasta maker with some flour, roll the dough with the roller compartment, from the highest settings to lowest.
  • 9.) After it got thinned, run it through the fettuccine cutter.
  • 10.) If it sticks to each other, dust it with a little bit of flour and break it apart.

    • Sauce

  • 1.) Crack the eggs, mix it well.
  • 2.) Grate the cheese, combine it with the eggs until it got to a thick consistency.
  • 3.) Prepare a stove top and a pot (filled with water), turn on the heat.
  • 4.) Salt the boiling water, start adding your pasta when the water reaches to a rapid boil (save some pasta water).
  • 5.) Stir it a bit, after its cooked drain the water.
  • 6.) Prepare a pan or a pot, turn on the stove to medium low heat.
  • 7.) Pour a little bit of oil if needed, cook your meat of choice.
  • 8.) After its cooked, turn off the heat. Add the cooked pasta in the pot, mix it with the sausages
  • 9.) Pour the eggs and cheese mixture into the pasta, mix throughly (feel free to turn on the heat if needed)
  • 10.) Serve it on a plate, garnish with some pepper and oregano.
